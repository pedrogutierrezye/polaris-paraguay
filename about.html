Our story

Built from the
ground up

Polaris S.A. was founded by professionals with backgrounds in finance, enterprise technology, and international business. Having lived and worked between Europe and Paraguay, we saw a clear gap: European capital and commercial interest in South America had no reliable, professionally run local counterpart.

We built Polaris to be that counterpart. A single, legally registered Sociedad Anónima in Paraguay that can import, export, invest, and build companies — without layers of intermediaries or offshore complexity.

Paraguay offers one of the most competitive environments in the world for business and investment: a flat 10% income tax, zero wealth tax, zero inheritance tax, strong property rights, and a dollarised real estate market. We help European partners access this environment with full legal clarity and local expertise.

We are based in Asunción. We know the market, the regulations, and the people. That is our edge.
