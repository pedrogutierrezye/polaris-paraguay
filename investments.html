Tax environment

One of the world's most
competitive tax regimes

Paraguay operates a territorial tax system — income earned outside Paraguay is not taxed. The domestic tax rates are among the lowest in the world, and there is no wealth, inheritance, or capital gains tax on most assets.

Income tax (flat)10%
Wealth tax0%

Legal framework

Secure property rights
for foreign buyers

Paraguay's constitution guarantees private property rights and permits foreign nationals to own property and businesses under the same conditions as Paraguayan citizens. All real estate transactions are registered through the Registro Público de la Propiedad, providing full legal title.

The Paraguayan guaraní is freely convertible. There are no restrictions on transferring capital in or out of the country, making it straightforward for European investors to repatriate returns.
