About us

Operators in
Paraguay,
connected to Europe

Polaris S.A. was founded by professionals with backgrounds in finance, technology, and international business. We are based in Asunción and operate directly — not as a broker or intermediary, but as the legal entity that executes.

Paraguay offers one of the most competitive tax environments in the world: a flat 10% income tax, zero wealth tax, zero inheritance tax, and no capital gains tax on most assets. We help European partners access this environment with full legal clarity.
