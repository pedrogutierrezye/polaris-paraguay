Inkubeitor · Venture Studio

Inkubeitor is the venture studio arm of Polaris S.A. We build, fund, and scale technology companies in Paraguay and the broader Latin American market.

What we do

A venture studio,
not a fund

Inkubeitor is not a passive investor. We are builders. We identify market problems, design business models, form founding teams, and build companies from the ground up — or accelerate existing ventures that need capital, structure, and expertise.

Our operating context is Paraguay: an underserved market with a growing middle class, low digital penetration, and significant white space in fintech, marketplaces, and business software.

Where we focus

Sectors with the
most opportunity

Fintech

Banking penetration in Paraguay remains low. Digital financial services are a large and growing opportunity.

Marketplaces

E-commerce and platform businesses are early-stage in Paraguay — the infrastructure gap is an advantage for first movers.

B2B Software

Paraguayan SMEs are underserved by local software. ERP, CRM, and vertical SaaS represent clear demand.

AI & Automation

Applying AI to local business problems — logistics, agriculture, finance — where the technology gap creates real leverage.

Agritech

Paraguay is one of the world's largest soy and beef exporters. Technology applied to the agricultural chain has enormous potential.
