Why Paraguay

A strategic hub for
South American trade

Central location

Paraguay borders Brazil, Argentina, and Bolivia — the three largest economies in South America. It is a natural distribution hub for the continent.

Low import tariffs

Paraguay applies some of the lowest import duties in the region. The country is a member of MERCOSUR, providing preferential access to the broader South American market.

Registered entity on the ground

Polaris S.A. is a legally registered Sociedad Anónima (RUC 80078960-1) with full import and export authorisation in Paraguay. No intermediaries required.

Competitive costs

Labour, logistics, and operational costs in Paraguay are significantly lower than in Brazil or Argentina, making it an attractive base for regional trade operations.
